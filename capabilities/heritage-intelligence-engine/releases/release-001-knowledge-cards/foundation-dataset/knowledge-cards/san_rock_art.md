# San Rock Art

## Category
Indigenous Heritage

## Location
Various regions across South Africa

## Summary
San Rock Art consists of ancient paintings created by the San people over thousands of years. These artworks provide valuable insight into early spiritual beliefs, daily life, hunting practices, and storytelling traditions.

## Key Facts

- Created thousands of years ago
- Found throughout Southern Africa
- Depicts animals, ceremonies, and hunting
- Protected as cultural heritage

## Cultural Significance

San Rock Art preserves one of Africa's oldest artistic traditions and reflects the deep spiritual and cultural knowledge of indigenous communities.
