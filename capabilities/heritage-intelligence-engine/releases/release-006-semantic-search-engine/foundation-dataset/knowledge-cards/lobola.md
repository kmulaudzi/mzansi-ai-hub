# Lobola

## Category
Cultural Tradition

## Location
Southern Africa

## Summary
Lobola is a traditional marriage custom practiced by many Southern African communities. It involves an agreed exchange, traditionally in cattle and today often in monetary form, between the families of the bride and groom as part of the marriage process.

## Key Facts

- Practised across many Southern African cultures
- Traditionally involved cattle
- Represents the union of two families
- Guided by cultural customs and family negotiations

## Cultural Significance

Lobola symbolises respect, family unity, commitment, and the strengthening of relationships between families. Practices vary across different cultures and communities.
