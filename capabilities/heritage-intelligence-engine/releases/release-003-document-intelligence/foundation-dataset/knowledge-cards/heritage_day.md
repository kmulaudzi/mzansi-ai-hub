# Heritage Day

## Category
National Commemoration

## Location
South Africa

## Summary
Heritage Day is celebrated annually on 24 September to recognise South Africa's diverse cultures, traditions, languages, and shared history.

## Key Facts

- Celebrated on 24 September
- National public holiday
- Promotes cultural diversity
- Celebrated through food, music, dance, and traditional clothing

## Cultural Significance

Heritage Day encourages South Africans to celebrate both their unique cultural identities and the unity that exists within the country's diversity.
