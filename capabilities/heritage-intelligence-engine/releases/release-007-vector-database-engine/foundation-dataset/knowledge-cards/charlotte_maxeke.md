# Charlotte Maxeke

## Category
Historical Figure

## Location
South Africa

## Summary
Charlotte Maxeke was an educator, social activist, and one of South Africa's earliest advocates for women's rights and equal education.

## Key Facts

- First Black South African woman to earn a university degree
- Founder of the Bantu Women's League
- Advocate for education and social justice
- Played a major role in women's activism

## Cultural Significance

Charlotte Maxeke is remembered as a pioneer of women's empowerment, education, and social equality in South Africa.
