# Ndebele Art

## Category
Cultural Heritage

## Location
Mpumalanga and Gauteng, South Africa

## Summary
Ndebele Art is recognised for its bold geometric patterns and vibrant colours painted on homes and decorative objects. It reflects identity, creativity, and cultural pride.

## Key Facts

- Characterised by colourful geometric designs
- Traditionally painted by Ndebele women
- Reflects cultural identity
- Recognised internationally

## Cultural Significance

Ndebele Art celebrates creativity, family, and cultural identity while preserving traditions passed down through generations.
