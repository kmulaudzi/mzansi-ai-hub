# Heritage Intelligence Engine

## Overview

The Heritage Intelligence Engine is the first core capability of the Mzansi AI Hub platform.

Its purpose is to transform structured and unstructured heritage information into searchable, explainable, and intelligent knowledge.

This engine will gradually evolve from simple knowledge retrieval into a complete Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system capable of understanding heritage documents, images, audio, and other cultural resources.

---

## Mission

To digitize, preserve and make South African heritage knowledge accessible through Artificial Intelligence.

## Development Philosophy

Rather than attempting to build a complete AI system in one step, the engine is developed through incremental releases.

Each release introduces one new capability while preserving everything built previously.
