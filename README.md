
# Mzansi AI Hub

Mzansi AI Hub is an AI platform being developed to digitize, preserve, understand, and make South African heritage knowledge more accessible through artificial intelligence.

The long-term goal is to transform heritage books, reports, images, audio recordings, videos, research documents, and other cultural assets into an intelligent digital knowledge platform.

## Platform Vision

Mzansi AI Hub is being developed as a collection of connected AI capabilities that will eventually work together as a multimodal heritage intelligence platform.

The platform is intended to support:

- Heritage knowledge discovery
- Intelligent document search
- Audio transcription and understanding
- Image and visual heritage analysis
- Video intelligence
- Multimodal retrieval
- Educational and public-facing assistants
- Game and storytelling experiences grounded in heritage knowledge

## Current Capability

### Heritage Intelligence Engine

The first capability being developed is the Heritage Intelligence Engine.

Its purpose is to ingest, organise, search, retrieve, and generate answers from structured and unstructured heritage data.

This capability is being developed incrementally through multiple releases.
